About Us

A salon built on
craft & care

Founded in 2012, Luxe Nails & Spa has been a beloved corner of Lake Stevens for over a decade. We believe every visit should feel like a pause — a moment of calm in your day — and every set should leave you walking out with confidence.

Our team of licensed nail technicians are passionate about their art and committed to your safety, comfort, and satisfaction.

Clean & sanitized tools — Every instrument sterilized between every client, every time.
Premium products only — We use OPI, CND, and other professional-grade brands.
No-rush environment — Your appointment time is fully yours. We never double-book.
